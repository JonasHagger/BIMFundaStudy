LCA & Circularity

ConceptCard 4

Foundational — the "extensions" beyond green design.

LCA (DIN EN ISO 14040) life-cycle assessment — combine embodied + operational emissions.

MCI Material Circularity Indicator (0–1, Ellen MacArthur).

BCI Building Circularity Indicator = MCI + Disassembly Potential, aggregated product→system→building.

Enabled by material passports (DPP, DBL, MP, Gebäuderessourcenpass). IFC tools: Madaster, Concular.
